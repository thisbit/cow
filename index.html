Mesto žensk na Mrežnem muzeju MG+MSUM

Mrežni muzej MG+MSUM, je spletno dostopen arhiv kamor se od leta 2016 dalje postopoma shranjuje tudi video arhiv Mesta žensk. Zaradi zaščite avtorskih pravic je za dostop do nekaterih vsebin na Mrežnem muzeju potrebno prej kontaktirati MG+MSUM ali Mesto žensk.

City of Women on the Network Museum MG+MSUM

The Network Museum MG+MSUM is a web-accessible archive where, since 2016, the video archive of City of Women has been gradually stored. Due to copyright protection, access to some content on the Network Museum might require prior contact with MG+MSUM or the City of Women.
